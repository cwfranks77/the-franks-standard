# Shipping and Returns Policy

**Shipping deadline**
- Sellers must ship within **7 business days** of order confirmation unless otherwise stated.

**Return window**
- Buyers may request returns within **30 days** of delivery.

**Remedies**
- Non-shipment: buyer may open a dispute after 7 business days; seller has 72 hours to provide tracking.
- Return refusal: seller must respond to RMA within 5 business days; marketplace may escalate.

**Money Back Guarantee**
- 100% refund for verified non-shipment, counterfeit, gross misdescription, or verified transit damage.
- Exclusions: buyer remorse, change of mind, minor subjective differences.

**Funding**
- Marketplace may hold a small fee (1–2%) from each sale to fund immediate refunds and place holds on seller payouts until disputes clear.
