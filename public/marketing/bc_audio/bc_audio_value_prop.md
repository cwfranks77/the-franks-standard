## Why Not Forums or Generic Marketplaces?

Generic marketplaces treat audio like any other SKU. Forums offer community but rarely offer structured refunds, tax compliance, or chain-of-custody. B&C Performance Audio sits in the middle: **community credibility plus platform enforcement.**

## Proof Points (Messaging-Ready)

- Multi-vendor marketplace infrastructure backed by The Franks Standard.
- COA uploads hashed, logged, and linked to listings and orders.
- Disputes with defined seller response windows and escalation paths.
- Sales tax calculated from shipping destination, not billing address.
- Separate brand presentation; unified safety and payments stack.

**Buyers:** Browse B&C Performance Audio on TFS—search by category, verify COA where enabled, checkout only on-platform.
