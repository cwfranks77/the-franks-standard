# Authenticity Banner

**Real items. Real proof. Zero tolerance for fakes.**

Listings do not go live without authenticity documentation. COAs issued on TFS carry serial numbers and chain-of-custody logs. Buyers can report suspicious items; severe cases open fraud investigations.

Counterfeits are not “buyer beware” — they are **platform violations** with refund, freeze, and ban consequences.

**Verify before you trust.** Look up COA serials. Read seller policies. Use authenticity reports when something feels off.
