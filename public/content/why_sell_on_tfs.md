# Why Sell on The Franks Standard

## Lower fees

TFS uses transparent transaction fees (typically **5% starter / 4.5% pro / 4% store tier**) — competitive with major marketplaces that also charge listing upgrades, promoted placements, and payment processing surprises. See `/pricing` for current public rates.

## Safer environment

- Policy signature required before you list
- Violation scanner protects you from abusive buyers in messages
- Dispute workflow with evidence — not endless chargeback chaos
- Account freeze tools remove bad actors who damage seller reputation

## COA system

Issue platform COAs with serial numbers buyers can verify. Chain-of-custody logging ties your listing photos and description to the certificate at issue time — reducing “bait and switch” claims.

## 3-month free store plan — Starter Free 90

New sellers who complete onboarding receive **Starter Free 90**:

- Full store access
- Unlimited listings
- Basic analytics
- **No commission tier changes** during the trial — your fee tier stays as configured

After 90 days, accounts move to the standard paid store plan automatically (backend switch — no action required).

## Daily spotlight system

One active TFS store is featured each day in the **daily spotlight** rotation. Consistent inventory, accurate photos, and policy compliance keep your shop eligible. Spotlight selection is random among active stores — giving small sellers real visibility.
