# Customer Service Quality — Our Commitment

## Follow-up email system

When a support ticket, phone case, or dispute is **closed**, TFS sends a follow-up email asking:

- How would you rate the experience? (1–5)
- Was your issue resolved? (yes / no / partial)
- Any additional comments?

Responses are stored in `support_ratings` for quality tracking — not deleted after a polite auto-reply.

## Rating system

Support ratings are tied to the case that triggered them. Duplicate submissions are blocked. Low scores with “unresolved” flags surface to owner oversight tools for human review.

## Resolution guarantees

We commit to:

- **Acknowledged disputes** — seller notification and status tracking
- **Documented outcomes** — rulings stored on the dispute record
- **Refund path** — verified seller-at-fault cases follow platform refund policy and Stripe mechanics
- **No silent closures** — closed cases trigger follow-up survey

We cannot guarantee every party will be happy with every ruling. We do guarantee the process is recorded and reviewable.

## Owner oversight

Charles Franks (owner) maintains direct oversight of escalated disputes, fraud cases, and law-enforcement report drafts. Owner tools read internal reporting guides and activity feeds — not automated bots sending your data externally.

## Abuse detection

Support channels are scanned like the rest of the platform. Users who weaponize tickets to harass, threaten, or scam support staff are subject to the same freeze and ban pipeline as marketplace abusers.
