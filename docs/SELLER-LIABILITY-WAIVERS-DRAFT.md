## Summary

Two separate electronic releases, signed **before a listing can publish**:

| Release | When required | Listing types |
|---------|---------------|---------------|
| **Form A** — Collectible, Antique & COA Liability Release | Collectibles, antiques, memorabilia, and any listing using uploaded COA or **serialized COA** | `listingRequiresCoa` categories + all `coa_type` paths |
| **Form B** — General Merchandise Seller Liability Release | Standard retail categories (electronics, tools, apparel, etc.) | General merchandise path on `/sell` |

**Serialized COA** = The Franks Standard LLC serialized COA with unique serial (`FS-YYYY-NNNNNN`), Seller Written Guarantee digitally attached in registry.

## Form A — Collectible, Antique & Certificate of Authenticity Liability Release

**THE FRANKS STANDARD LLC — MARKETPLACE FACILITATOR**  
**COLLECTIBLE, ANTIQUE & COA SELLER LIABILITY RELEASE AND ACKNOWLEDGMENT**  
Version 2026-06-12

### 1. Parties and role of the Platform

This Collectible, Antique & COA Seller Liability Release and Acknowledgment (“**Release**”) is entered into between **The Franks Standard LLC**, a Louisiana limited liability company (“**Platform**,” “**we**,” “**us**”), acting solely as a **marketplace facilitator**, and the undersigned seller (“**Seller**,” “**you**”).

The Platform **does not** buy, sell, own, possess, inspect, authenticate, grade, warrant, or guarantee any item listed on the marketplace facilitator website operated by **The Franks Standard LLC** (thefranksstandard.com). The Platform provides listing tools, checkout facilitation, escrow coordination, policy enforcement, and—when you elect it—a **serialized COA** template and serial registry issued by **The Franks Standard LLC**.

### 2. Seller sole responsibility for the item

Seller represents and agrees that Seller alone is responsible for:

- Truth and accuracy of the title, description, condition, category, photos, and price;
- Legal right to sell the item;
- Compliance with all applicable laws (including consumer protection, intellectual property, and export rules);
- Authenticity, genuineness, provenance, and “as described” status of the item.

**If the item is counterfeit, fraudulent, stolen, materially misdescribed, the wrong item, or not as represented, Seller bears full responsibility** — not the Platform.

### 3. Certificates of authenticity — uploaded and serialized

#### 3.1 Uploaded third-party COA

If Seller uploads a third-party certificate (PSA, PCGS, issuer PDF, etc.), Seller represents that the file is genuine, unaltered, applicable to **this** item only, and that Seller—not the Platform—backs the certificate’s accuracy. The Platform **does not** verify, endorse, or guarantee third-party certificate content.

#### 3.2 Serialized COA (The Franks Standard LLC)

If Seller uses a **serialized COA** issued through **The Franks Standard LLC** marketplace facilitator website:

- One unique serial (`FS-YYYY-NNNNNN`) binds to **one** listing snapshot (photos and description frozen at issue);
- Seller’s **Seller Written Guarantee** is digitally attached to that serial in the Platform registry;
- The **serialized COA is a record-keeping and disclosure tool**, not a Platform warranty, certification, appraisal, or guarantee that the item is genuine;
- Seller **may not** reuse, transfer, or reassign a serial to a different item or listing.

Seller expressly acknowledges that **any use of a serialized COA downloaded from, completed on, or issued through thefranksstandard.com, the marketplace facilitator website of The Franks Standard LLC, does not create any liability for The Franks Standard LLC** if the item is later found inauthentic, fraudulent, or not as described.

### 4. No Platform liability — buyer and seller

Seller agrees that **disputes about authenticity, condition, description, delivery, or refunds are primarily between Seller and the buyer**. Seller will not name **The Franks Standard LLC** or the Platform as a party, co-defendant, or insurer in any claim arising from Seller’s listing, COA, or item except where applicable law **mandatorily** imposes facilitator duties that cannot be waived.

To the fullest extent permitted by Louisiana law and applicable federal law, Seller **releases, waives, and discharges** **The Franks Standard LLC**, the Platform, and its members, managers, employees, and agents from any and all claims, demands, damages, losses, and expenses (including attorneys’ fees) arising out of or related to:

- Authenticity or genuineness of any item Seller lists;
- Any uploaded COA or **serialized COA** Seller uses;
- Misrepresentation, fraud, or counterfeiting by Seller;
- Buyer dissatisfaction with item condition or description when Seller is at fault.

This Release **does not** limit non-waivable consumer rights of buyers against Seller, or the Platform’s right to enforce Marketplace Policies (listing removal, refunds advanced to buyers, account freeze, ban, debt recovery).

### 5. Indemnification

Seller shall **defend, indemnify, and hold harmless** **The Franks Standard LLC** and the Platform from any third-party claim, investigation, regulatory action, or litigation arising from Seller’s listings, items, COAs (uploaded or serialized), or conduct, including claims that an item was inauthentic or fraudulent.

### 6. Policy enforcement (not a warranty)

Seller understands that Platform enforcement (refunds, COA revocation, account action) is **risk reduction and policy application**, not an admission that the Platform guaranteed authenticity at the time of sale.

### 7. Electronic signature

By typing your full legal name and confirming below, you agree this Release is signed electronically under the E-SIGN Act and Louisiana law, with the same force as ink, and you consent to storage of your signature record in the Platform database.

**Required acknowledgment (checkbox):**  
“I have read and agree to the Collectible, Antique & COA Seller Liability Release. I accept **full legal responsibility** for my item and any COA I use (uploaded or **serialized COA**). I understand The Franks Standard LLC is a marketplace facilitator only and has **no liability** for authenticity, fraud, or misdescription of my listings.”

## Form B — General Merchandise Seller Liability Release

**THE FRANKS STANDARD LLC — MARKETPLACE FACILITATOR**  
**GENERAL MERCHANDISE SELLER LIABILITY RELEASE**  
Version 2026-06-12

### 1. Parties

Between **The Franks Standard LLC** (“**Platform**”), marketplace facilitator only, and the undersigned seller (“**Seller**”).

### 2. Seller sole responsibility

For non-collectible general merchandise listings (electronics, tools, apparel, appliances, and similar retail categories **not** requiring COA), Seller alone is responsible for:

- Accurate title, description, condition, photos, and price;
- Legal right to sell;
- Shipping as described and honoring buyer remedies when Seller is at fault.

The Platform does not inspect, test, or warrant items.

### 3. No COA path

This Release applies to listings **without** collectible COA requirements. If Seller later changes category or wording to collectible/antique, Seller must complete **Form A** before publish.

### 4. Limitation of liability and buyer-seller disputes

Seller agrees buyer disputes about wrong item, not as described, defective, or undelivered goods are **between Seller and buyer**, and that **The Franks Standard LLC** shall not be involved as a party to such disputes except as mandatory law requires. Seller releases **The Franks Standard LLC** and the Platform from claims arising from Seller’s merchandise listings to the fullest extent permitted by law, except mandatory facilitator obligations.

Seller **indemnifies** **The Franks Standard LLC** and the Platform against third-party claims arising from Seller’s general merchandise listings.

### 5. Electronic signature

Same E-SIGN consent as Form A.

**Required acknowledgment (checkbox):**  
“I have read and agree to the General Merchandise Seller Liability Release. I accept **full legal responsibility** for my listing. The Franks Standard LLC is a marketplace facilitator only and is **not liable** for my item descriptions, condition, or fulfillment.”

## Technical alignment (proposed — not implemented)

### What you already have

- `seller_policy_acceptances` — account-level Terms + Marketplace Policy bundle (`useSellerPolicyAcceptance.ts`)
- Checkbox on `/sell` for serialized COA acknowledgment (`coaFranksAck`) — **not stored as durable signed release today**
- `coa_certificates` table — serial, fingerprint, listing binding
- `docs/AUTHENTICITY-SEAL-LIABILITY.md` — internal product language

### Proposed addition

**Table:** `seller_liability_releases`

| Column | Purpose |
|--------|---------|
| `id` | UUID |
| `seller_id` | Who signed |
| `release_type` | `collectible_antique_coa` \| `general_merchandise` |
| `policy_version` | e.g. `2026-06-12` |
| `signer_legal_name` | Typed signature |
| `listing_id` | Set at publish (nullable until then) |
| `coa_certificate_id` | Link when serialized COA issued |
| `coa_serial_number` | e.g. `FS-2026-000042` |
| `serialized_coa_used` | boolean |
| `release_text_sha256` | Hash of exact legal text signed |
| `signature_sha256` | Hash of name + version + timestamp + seller_id |
| `ip_address`, `user_agent` | Audit trail |
| `signed_at` | Timestamp |

**Gate before publish (sell.vue / API):**

1. General path → must have valid Form B for this listing attempt (or within session TTL).
2. Collectible/COA path → Form A required; if `franks_issued` → `serialized_coa_used = true` and link to certificate on issue.
3. RLS: listings `insert` blocked unless matching release exists for `seller_id` + `listing_id` or pending draft id.

**Free vs paid e-sign**

| Option | Cost | Notes |
|--------|------|-------|
| **Built-in (recommended)** | Free | Typed name + checkbox + SHA-256 + Supabase row — same pattern as `seller_policy_acceptances` |
| DocuSign | Paid | Stronger third-party audit trail; API + per-envelope fees |
| Formstack | Paid | Forms + signatures; monthly plans |

Recommendation: **built-in first** (free, you own the data). Attorney can later require DocuSign for high-value categories.
