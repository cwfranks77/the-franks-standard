Drop your phone recordings in THIS folder.

Name them (any audio format — m4a, mp3, wav):
  charles-raw.m4a   (or charles-raw.mp3, etc.)
  brandy-raw.m4a

Then on your computer run:
  cd C:\Users\ninja\dev\the-franks-standard
  .\scripts\enhance-greeting-audio.ps1

That creates the cleaned files:
  charles-greeting.mp3
  brandy-greeting.mp3

If you stumbled over words, re-record that clip — automatic tools cannot fix wrong words,
only background noise, volume, and long silences.

After enhancement, tell your developer to set Supabase secrets and redeploy the voice function.
