# Buyer Safety Tips

Practical steps to shop safely on The Franks Standard and the B&C Performance Audio lane.

---

## Golden Rules

1. **Pay only through TFS checkout**
2. **Keep communication on-platform**
3. **Report** off-platform payment requests immediately
4. **Do not confront** suspected scammers—use disputes and reports
5. **Inspect orders promptly** and document condition on arrival

---

## Before Purchase

- Research fair market price—deals too good to be true often are
- Read full listing and all photos
- Check COA and seller reviews on high-trust items
- Message for extra serial photos if needed—stay in app chat

---

## At Checkout

- Verify **shipping ZIP** — sales tax is calculated from shipping destination (Louisiana marketplace rules)
- Confirm item, quantity, and total before paying
- Save order confirmation email or screenshot

---

## After Purchase

- Track shipment with provided tracking number
- Video or photo unpack for expensive gear (optional but helpful in disputes)
- Contact seller on-platform first for minor issues (missing manual, etc.)
- Open dispute if material problem; don't accept "fix it off eBay"

---

## Scam Patterns to Watch

| Pattern | Your response |
|---------|----------------|
| "Friends & Family for discount" | Refuse; report listing/message |
| Redirect to WhatsApp/Telegram for pay | Stay on TFS; report |
| Fake tracking link in email | Open tracking from order page only |
| Seller asks to cancel order and wire | Refuse; dispute if already paid |

---

## Personal Information

- Never send passwords, SSN, or full card numbers in messages
- Platform checkout handles payment—you don't need to "verify card" via DM

---

## Local Pickup

If ever offered outside standard shipped checkout, treat off-platform handoff as **unprotected** unless explicitly covered by current written policy. Default: ship through platform flow.

---

## AI & Support

- AI chat answers FAQs about orders, COA, and disputes
- Escalations reach **owner review** for complex fraud—not instant bot refunds
- Have order ID ready when contacting support

---

## B&C Buyers

Competition audio has a culture of handshake deals at shows. On TFS, **finish the deal on checkout** even if you met the seller in person—tax, receipt, and dispute path depend on it.
