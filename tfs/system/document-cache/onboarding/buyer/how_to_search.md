# How to Search on The Franks Standard

Find authentic gear quickly using search, filters, and seller signals.

---

## Basic Search

Use the **top search bar** on the marketplace home:

1. Enter keywords — model name, brand, category term (e.g. "12 sub DVC", "vintage receiver")
2. Press Enter or tap search
3. Browse results in the grid layout

Tips:

- Use specific model numbers over generic terms
- Try alternate spellings or abbreviations if results are thin
- Check both main marketplace and B&C lane for competition audio

---

## Filters & Sorting

Where available, narrow results by:

| Filter | Use when |
|--------|----------|
| Category | You know the item type |
| Price range | Budget-bound |
| Condition | You only want new or used |
| COA / authenticity | High-trust purchase |
| Seller rating | You want established sellers |
| Shipping | You need fast or free shipping |

**Sort** by relevance, price low/high, or newest listings depending on your goal.

---

## Reading a Result Card

Before clicking, note:

- **Price** and shipping hint
- **Condition** label
- **Seller badge** or store name
- **COA** or trust indicators on eligible categories
- **Review average** if shown

---

## Listing Page Deep Dive

Open the listing and scroll:

- All photos (zoom flaws)
- Full description and included accessories
- Seller policies (returns where offered)
- COA section and serial when present
- Seller's other listings for consistency

---

## Saved Searches & Alerts

If your account supports saved searches:

- Save frequent queries (model + condition)
- Enable alerts for new matches—reduces missing rare gear

---

## Messages Before Purchase

You may message sellers with **on-platform chat**:

- Ask for extra photos of serial or wear
- Confirm what's included
- Do not agree to off-platform payment for any reason

---

## B&C Performance Audio

Competition audio buyers should:

- Search within the B&C lane for SPL-focused inventory
- Verify power ratings and impedance in description vs photos
- Treat COA and seller history as seriously as on main TFS

---

## After You Find It

- Add to cart or buy now through **checkout only**
- Confirm **shipping ZIP** before paying—sales tax is based on destination
