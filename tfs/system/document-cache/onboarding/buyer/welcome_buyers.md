# Welcome, Buyers

Welcome to **The Franks Standard**—a multi-vendor marketplace for **authentic gear**, from collectibles and specialty items to audio equipment and the **B&C Performance Audio** competition lane.

---

## Why Shop Here

- **On-platform checkout only** — your payment is documented and dispute-ready
- **COA chain-of-custody** on eligible high-trust listings
- **Fraud monitoring** — report off-platform payment requests
- **Reviews and seller history** — see who you're buying from
- **Louisiana-compliant sales tax** — calculated from your **shipping ZIP** at checkout

---

## Getting Started

1. **Create an account** with a secure password
2. **Add your shipping address** — tax and delivery quotes use your shipping destination
3. **Search** by keyword, category, or seller
4. **Checkout on TFS** — never pay a seller directly off-platform

---

## What Makes a Good Purchase

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| Before buy | Read listing, view all photos, check COA badge if present |
| During checkout | Confirm shipping ZIP, review tax and total |
| After delivery | Inspect promptly; leave an honest review |
| If problem | Open dispute from **My Orders** with photos |

---

## B&C Performance Audio

Looking for competition subs, amps, and SPL gear? Browse the **B&C Performance Audio** lane—same checkout and protection as the main marketplace, with a catalog focused on performance audio. Qualified sellers there may offer lower platform fees; your buyer protection does not change.

---

## Your Safety

- Report **Report authenticity** or support if something feels wrong
- Do not confront suspected scammers—use platform tools
- AI chat helps with FAQs; escalations reach owner review when needed
