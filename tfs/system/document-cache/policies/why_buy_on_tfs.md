# Why Buy on The Franks Standard

## Authenticity guarantee culture

Sellers must provide proof — COA, signed guarantee, or documented grading — before listings go live. That is the opposite of “scroll and hope.” If something looks wrong, use the **authenticity report** tools on any listing.

## Safer messaging

Messages run through the platform send pipeline with:

- PII and scam-pattern scanning
- Violation enforcement (freeze or ban for abusers)
- Mandatory contact verification for messaging

You should not need to leave the site to “complete the deal” — that is a classic scam pattern we block.

## Verified sellers

Sellers connect Stripe accounts, accept signed marketplace policies, and remain subject to integrity holds and freezes. Repeat counterfeit or fraud behavior results in bans — not a new username.

## Review system

After a **verified purchase**, buyers can leave seller reviews. Sellers can review buyers on completed orders. Everyone can rate the platform itself. All review text passes the violation scanner.

## Dispute protection

Paid orders support structured disputes with evidence upload. Cases can escalate to TFS review. Resolved cases trigger follow-up surveys so service quality is measured — not guessed.

## Checkout built for trust

- Tax calculated on **shipping destination** (Louisiana marketplace facilitator compliant)
- Stripe-powered payment flow
- Activity logging for your account security
- Contact info and policy acceptance required before purchase

| Elsewhere | On TFS |

| DM for payment | Escrow-style checkout |
| Report button → void | Dispute + authenticity pipelines |

Buy collectibles and gear with proof — not promises.
