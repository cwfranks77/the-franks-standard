# COA — Certificate of Authenticity

## What is COA?

COA is our **chain-of-custody** system for high-trust listings. Uploads are hashed, logged, and linked to listings and orders.

## Serial numbers

Serialized COA items receive unique serial numbers at certificate issue.

## Verification

Buyers can verify COA serials through platform verification tools where enabled.

## Uploads

Sellers upload COA evidence during listing creation or post-sale as required by category policy.
