# Platform Overview

## What is The Franks Standard?

The Franks Standard is a multi-vendor marketplace focused on **authentic gear** — collectibles, audio equipment, and specialty items — with built-in fraud protection, COA chain-of-custody, and Louisiana-compliant tax handling.

## How do I get started?

- **Buyers**: Create an account, search listings, pay only through platform checkout.
- **Sellers**: Connect Stripe, accept seller policies, list with clear photos and descriptions.

## Fees

Default platform fee is 10%. B&C Performance Audio sellers on the linked store may qualify for reduced fees. Featured and starter plans may differ — see seller onboarding.

## Support

Use in-app messaging, disputes, and support tickets. AI chat can answer FAQs; escalations go to owner review.
