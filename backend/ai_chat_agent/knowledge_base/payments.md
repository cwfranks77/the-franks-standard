# Payments

## Checkout

All purchases must use The Franks Standard checkout. Never pay a seller directly off-platform.

## Sales tax

Tax is calculated from the **shipping destination ZIP code** (Louisiana marketplace facilitator rules).

## Fees

Platform fees are deducted at sale. Sellers receive net payouts per payout schedule.

## Refunds

Refunds go through the dispute and refund request system. Outcomes are decided per policy — AI cannot promise amounts or timelines.
