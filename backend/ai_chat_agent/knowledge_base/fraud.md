# Fraud

## Reporting fraud

Use **Report authenticity** or contact support with order ID, listing link, and screenshots.

## What we monitor

- Counterfeit claims
- Off-platform payment requests
- Suspicious messaging
- Device and IP risk signals

## Escalation

Serious fraud may become a **fraud case** reviewed by the owner. Law enforcement reports are prepared manually — never auto-sent.

## Your safety

Do not confront suspected scammers. Use platform tools only.
