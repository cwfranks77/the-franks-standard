# Disputes

## How to open a dispute

Go to **My Orders**, select the order, and choose **Open dispute**. Describe the issue and attach evidence.

## Seller response

Sellers have a limited window to respond. If unresolved, the case may escalate to platform review.

## Outcomes

Disputes may resolve in favor of the buyer or seller. AI support cannot override owner rulings.

## Refunds

Approved refunds are processed through Stripe per platform policy.
