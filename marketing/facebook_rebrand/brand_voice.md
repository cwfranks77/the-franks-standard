# Brand Voice — The Franks Standard (Facebook)

**Tone:** Direct, confident, protective — not corporate fluff.

**We say:** proof, verify, escrow, enforce, protect, authentic, documented, Louisiana-built.

**We avoid:** "amazing deals," "hurry," "DM for price," "trust me bro," hype without substance.

**Audience:** Collectors, coin/card sellers, gear flippers, Louisiana small businesses, competition audio buyers.

**BC Audio voice (subset):** Technical, SPL-aware, wholesale-direct, competition-grade — still honest, no exaggeration on wattage claims.
