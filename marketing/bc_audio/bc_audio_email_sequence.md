## Email 2 — Trust & COA (Day 3)

**Subject:** How we protect competition buyers (and honest sellers)

**Preview:** COA, fraud signals, and why off-platform pay is banned.

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

Competition audio has a long history of handshake deals. B&C Performance Audio keeps the community energy—but adds structure.

**Certificate of Authenticity (COA)**
For high-trust listings, sellers upload COA evidence during listing or as category policy requires. Files are **hashed and logged** and linked to listings and orders. Serialized certificates receive unique numbers buyers can verify where verification is enabled.

**Fraud protection**
We monitor counterfeit claims, off-platform payment requests, and risky messaging patterns. If something serious surfaces, it becomes a **fraud case** for owner review.

**Golden rule:** Never pay a seller outside TFS checkout. If anyone asks for Venmo, Zelle, or wire—report it.

**Buyers:** Open disputes from **My Orders** if an item isn't as described. Attach photos and messages.

**Sellers:** Respond to disputes within your window; accurate listings are your best defense.

Shop or list with confidence,
— The Franks Standard team

## Email 3 — Convert (Day 7)

**Subject:** Ready to move? Here's your B&C checklist.

**Preview:** Fees, payouts, and your first listing—or first protected purchase.

**Body:**

Hi {{first_name}},

One week in—here's how to get the most from B&C Performance Audio.

**For buyers**
1. Save searches for subs, amps, or brands you trust.
2. Check COA badges and seller history before checkout.
3. Use reviews after delivery—honest feedback helps the lane.

**For sellers**
1. Confirm Stripe is connected and payout details are current.
2. Photograph serial numbers and cosmetic flaws upfront.
3. Ask about **B&C fee qualification** if you haven't already.
4. Ship with tracking; upload COA docs when the category requires them.

**Tax note:** Sales tax is calculated from the buyer's **shipping destination ZIP code**, per Louisiana marketplace facilitator rules. You don't need to guess rates at checkout—we handle calculation on-platform.
