# B&C Performance Audio — Brand Story

## Origin

B&C Performance Audio was built for a different kind of buyer and seller: people who live in the lane of competition sound, SPL builds, and high-output mobile audio. Where The Franks Standard serves a broad multi-vendor marketplace for authentic gear across categories, B&C Performance Audio is a focused storefront inside the TFS ecosystem—same trust rails, same COA and fraud protection, but tuned for the competition audio community.

The name reflects a commitment to performance first: gear that earns its place on the meter, not just in a photo. Sellers who list here are builders, distributors, and serious hobbyists who understand that reputation in this space is earned in person, at shows, and on the wall.

## What We Stand For

- **Real competition gear** — amplifiers, subs, enclosures, wiring, and accessories meant for judged and metered environments.
- **Lower seller fees** — qualified B&C Performance Audio sellers may receive reduced platform fees compared to default TFS rates, so more margin stays with the people doing the work.
- **Shared trust infrastructure** — checkout, disputes, COA chain-of-custody, and Louisiana-compliant tax handling run on the same secure backbone as The Franks Standard. Buyers never trade safety for a niche catalog.

## The Relationship to The Franks Standard

B&C Performance Audio is not a separate website with different rules. It is a **brand lane** within TFS: dark-themed presentation, competition-oriented merchandising, and community language—but one owner-operated platform, one set of authenticity standards, and one obligation to protect buyers and honest sellers.

We do not blend identities in product files or checkout flows. Franks Standard remains the marketplace shell; B&C Performance Audio is where competition audio finds its home without leaving the ecosystem.

## Who This Is For

**Buyers** chasing verified builds, documented serials, and sellers who show up at events—not anonymous drop-shippers.

**Sellers** who want a serious venue for SPL and competition inventory, with fraud monitoring and structured payouts instead of forum deals and cash apps.

## Promise

If it is listed under B&C Performance Audio on TFS, it is held to the same authenticity, messaging, and payment rules as every other listing on the platform. The lane is specialized. The standard is not negotiable.
