## About section

The Franks Standard (TFS) is a Louisiana-based online marketplace built for collectors, resellers, and serious buyers who are tired of "buyer beware."

We require proof before publish — Certificate of Authenticity, signed seller guarantee, or documented grading — so listings are not just photos and promises.

**What we enforce:**
- COA chain-of-custody with verifiable serial numbers
- Violation scanner on messages and reviews
- Account freeze, fraud cases, and permanent bans
- Anti-rejoin fingerprinting
- Dispute resolution with owner oversight

**For sellers:** Starter Free 90 plan (3 months, full store, unlimited listings). Daily store spotlight. Transparent fees from 5% down.

**For buyers:** Escrow-style Stripe checkout, authenticity reports, verified seller policies, and structured disputes.

**B&C Performance Audio** — our featured competition car audio division at bcpoweraudio.com.

Founded by Charles Franks. Marketplace facilitator tax compliant (Louisiana Act 22).

Website: https://thefranksstandard.com
