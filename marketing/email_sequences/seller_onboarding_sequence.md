# Seller Onboarding Email Sequence

**Trigger:** Seller policies accepted  
**Do not auto-send until owner enables.**

---

## Email 1 — Day 0

**Subject:** Your Starter Free 90 store is active  
**Preview:** 3 months free — unlimited listings, full store access.

**Body:**  
You completed seller onboarding. Your **Starter Free 90** plan is active for 90 days.

**Included:** Full store · Unlimited listings · Basic analytics · No commission tier changes during trial

**Next:** Connect Stripe → add inventory → attach proof to each listing → publish

After 90 days you'll move to Marketplace Standard automatically (backend switch).

**CTA:** Open seller dashboard → /sell

**Timing:** Immediate on policy acceptance

---

## Email 2 — Day 3

**Subject:** Proof gate: what documents you need  
**Preview:** COA, signed guarantee, or grading — pick your path.

**Body:**  
Listings cannot publish without authenticity documentation.

- Issue a platform COA (serial + chain-of-custody)  
- Upload third-party grading with matching photos  
- Signed seller guarantee per category rules

Counterfeits = refunds, freezes, bans. Zero tolerance.

**CTA:** Issue COA guide → /learn/tools/authenticity-checklist

**Timing:** 3 days after onboarding

---

## Email 3 — Day 14

**Subject:** Get featured in daily store spotlight  
**Preview:** One active store highlighted every day.

**Body:**  
Keep your store active, accurate photos, and policy compliance — you're eligible for **daily spotlight**, a random featured shop rotation.

Also: aim for Seller Excellence (#1 each cycle can earn 0% platform fees for 30 days).

**CTA:** View pricing & excellence → /pricing

**Timing:** 14 days after onboarding

---

## Email 4 — Day 80

**Subject:** 10 days left on Starter Free 90  
**Preview:** Your store plan switches automatically on day 91.

**Body:**  
Your free trial ends in 10 days. You'll move to **Marketplace Standard** store plan — no action needed unless you want to review pricing.

Keep your Connect account current so payouts continue smoothly.

**CTA:** Review plans → /pricing

**Timing:** 80 days after trial start
