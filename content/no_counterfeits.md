# No Counterfeits — Zero Tolerance Policy

## Our position

The Franks Standard **does not allow** knowingly counterfeit, replica, or misrepresented goods. “Reproduction,” “inspired by,” or “style” items must be labeled clearly and priced accordingly — and still may be removed if they violate category rules or infringe rights.

## Enforcement methods

1. **Proof gate** — listings without acceptable authenticity documentation do not publish.
2. **Authenticity reports** — buyers and community members flag suspicious items; reports are scanned and queued for review.
3. **Violation scanner** — listing text and messages scanned for counterfeit keywords and fraud patterns.
4. **Integrity holds** — seller accounts restricted while investigations run.
5. **Forced refunds** — seller-at-fault refunds create platform debt and can trigger freezes.
6. **Permanent bans** — repeat or severe offenders are removed with anti-rejoin fingerprinting.

## COA requirements

Platform-issued COAs include:

- Unique serial number (`FS-YYYY-######`)
- Listing fingerprint at time of issue (photos + title + description hash)
- Chain-of-custody log entries for upload, issue, and transfer events

Third-party grading (PCGS, NGC, PSA, etc.) must match the item shown — mismatches are treated as misrepresentation.

## Reporting process

1. Open the listing and submit an **authenticity report** with details and photos.
2. Choose the reason: suspected counterfeit, misrepresented grade, wrong photos, fake COA document, or other.
3. TFS reviews evidence; severe cases open fraud cases automatically.
4. Buyers on affected paid orders may receive refunds when investigations uphold the report.

## Law enforcement escalation

Counterfeits involving currency, federal offenses, or organized fraud may be escalated per the owner reporting guide:

- FBI IC3: https://www.ic3.gov/
- U.S. Secret Service field offices: https://www.secretservice.gov/contact/field-offices
- Local police via https://www.usa.gov/local-governments

TFS prepares evidence packages for owner review — **we do not auto-report to agencies**.

---

Fake products are not a “category.” They are a violation. Full stop.
