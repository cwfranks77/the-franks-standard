# Safety Banner

**Your account. Your data. Our enforcement.**

The Franks Standard runs 24/7 backend safety: violation scanning, fraud cases, account freezes, permanent bans, and anti-rejoin fingerprinting. VPN and datacenter signups may require phone verification. Messaging scams trigger automatic freezes — not warnings buried in email.

**Disputes** follow a documented path from open → response → TFS review → resolution, with follow-up quality surveys after close.

Shop and sell where abuse has consequences — not unlimited second chances.
