# Seller Best Practices

Practical habits that improve sales, reduce disputes, and keep your account healthy on The Franks Standard.

---

## Listings That Sell and Stay Sold

1. **Lead with honesty** — condition in the title or first line ("Used – light rack wear")
2. **Six-plus photos** — hero, back, serial, flaws, accessories
3. **Specific compatibility** — model years, ohm load, enclosure volume for audio gear
4. **Ship-from accuracy** — wrong ZIP causes tax/shipping disputes

---

## COA & High-Trust Categories

- Upload COA before going live when required
- Match serial on cert to serial on item
- For B&C competition gear, document RMS ratings truthfully—buyers know the specs

---

## Pricing

- Research completed sales on-platform, not fantasy forum prices
- Factor platform fee into margin upfront
- Use clear shipping policy—surprise shipping costs cause INAD disputes

---

## Communication

- Reply within 24 hours when possible
- Confirm address and special instructions in on-platform messages
- Never move payment or warranty claims off-platform

---

## Packing & Shipping

- Double-box fragile audio and collectibles
- Photo the packed item before seal
- Upload tracking the same day you ship
- Insure high-value shipments when carrier allows

---

## After the Sale

- Mark shipped promptly
- Follow up once if delivery stalls (carrier issue, not buyer blame)
- Politely ask for a review after successful delivery—don't incentivize fake reviews

---

## Dispute Prevention

| Buyer concern | Your move |
|---------------|-----------|
| "Is this authentic?" | Point to COA badge and serial photos pre-sale |
| "Can you do $X off-platform?" | Decline; report if harassment |
| "Item differs from photos" | Prevent with better photos upfront |

---

## Account Hygiene

- One primary seller account per business unless approved
- Rotate passwords; don't share login
- Review **Seller Dashboard** weekly for policy updates

---

## B&C Performance Audio Lane

- Qualify for reduced fees if eligible—still list with TFS authenticity standards
- Use competition-specific keywords buyers search (SPL, amp model, voice coil size)
- Marketing is dark-themed B&C; your listing discipline is still Franks-grade

---

## When to Contact Support

- Buyer requests something that feels like fraud
- You uploaded wrong COA before shipment
- Payout hold you don't understand with order ID ready
