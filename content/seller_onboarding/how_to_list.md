# How to List on The Franks Standard

Step-by-step guide for creating a listing that meets authenticity standards and converts buyers.

---

## Step 1 — Prepare Your Item

- Clean the item and photograph in good light
- Capture **all sides**, labels, serial numbers, and any flaws
- Gather COA paperwork if the category requires chain-of-custody
- Confirm you have the right to sell (ownership, no liens, not prohibited)

---

## Step 2 — Start a New Listing

From **Seller Dashboard → Listings → Create listing**:

1. Select **category** (determines COA and attribute requirements)
2. Enter **title** — model name, key spec, condition keyword (no misleading hype)
3. Write **description** — honest condition, what's included, what's not, shipping notes

---

## Step 3 — Photos & Media

Minimum expectations:

- Main image: clear hero shot on neutral background
- Detail shots: serial plate, wear, accessories
- No watermarks that obscure the item
- No stock photos for used items

Poor photos drive disputes. Invest five extra minutes here.

---

## Step 4 — Pricing & Inventory

- Set **price** in USD unless your store config specifies otherwise
- Set **quantity** if you have multiples
- Consider shipping cost strategy (calculated vs flat)—be explicit in description

Platform fee is deducted at sale; see your dashboard for your rate (default 10%, plans may vary).

---

## Step 5 — COA & Authenticity Fields

If the category shows **COA required**:

1. Upload certificate images or PDFs
2. Enter serial numbers when prompted
3. Do not submit another seller's certificate or reused serial

See [How to use COA](how_to_use_coa.md) for detail.

---

## Step 6 — Shipping

- Enter **ship-from ZIP** accurately (affects quotes and compliance)
- Choose allowed carriers/methods per policy
- State handling time honestly (e.g. ships within 2 business days)

Buyer's **shipping destination ZIP** drives sales tax at their checkout—not your location alone.

---

## Step 7 — Review & Publish

Checklist before submit:

- [ ] Title matches photos
- [ ] Condition matches reality
- [ ] COA uploaded if required
- [ ] No "contact me off-platform for discount" language
- [ ] Prohibited items rules cleared

After publish, monitor messages and orders from **Seller Dashboard**.

---

## Editing & Ending Listings

- **Edit** for typos, additional photos, or price changes—major changes on active orders may require buyer communication
- **End** when sold out elsewhere or item unavailable—never sell the same unit twice

---

## Common Mistakes

| Mistake | Fix |
|---------|-----|
| Vague condition ("good") | Use specific notes: scratches, dents, tested/working |
| Missing serial photo | Add before a buyer asks |
| Off-platform pay hint | Remove; risk of suspension |
| Wrong category | Recreate listing to apply correct COA rules |
