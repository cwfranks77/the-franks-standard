# How to Avoid Violations

Staying in good standing protects your payouts, reputation, and access to the marketplace. These are the most common violations and how to avoid them.

---

## Critical Violations (Zero Tolerance)

### Off-Platform Payments

**Don't:** Ask for Venmo, Zelle, PayPal Friends & Family, wire, or cash to "save fees."  
**Do:** Direct every buyer to TFS checkout only.

**Why:** Buyers lose protection; you risk immediate suspension and fraud review.

### Counterfeits & Misrepresentation

**Don't:** List replicas as authentic, wrong model numbers, or hide damage.  
**Do:** Disclose flaws; upload COA when required; photograph serials.

### COA Fraud

**Don't:** Reuse certificates, fake serials, or upload docs that don't match the item.  
**Do:** Upload genuine chain-of-custody per [How to use COA](how_to_use_coa.md).

---

## High-Risk Violations

### Shill Bidding or Review Manipulation

No fake accounts, compensated reviews without disclosure, or retaliation reviews.

### Harassment

Professional messages only—no threats, slurs, or doxxing in disputes.

### Prohibited Items

Check category and platform prohibited lists (stolen goods, recalled items where banned, etc.).

### Circumventing Fees

Multi-account schemes, redirecting repeat buyers off-platform, or listing only to harvest leads violates policy.

---

## Operational Violations

| Issue | Prevention |
|-------|------------|
| Late shipment | Set realistic handling time; ship with tracking |
| Item not as described | Match photos and condition grade |
| Ignoring disputes | Respond in your window with tracking and photos |
| Stock photos for used gear | Use your own images |
| Wrong ship-from ZIP | Update store settings for accurate tax/shipping |

---

## Messaging Rules

- Keep transaction coordination on-platform
- Don't share personal banking for "direct deposit" from buyers
- Report suspicious buyers instead of arguing

---

## Account Health

Warnings may precede freezes for fixable issues. Pattern abuse, fraud, or critical violations can lead to:

- Listing removal
- Payout hold
- Account freeze or ban
- IP/device ban (severe cases)

Owner review applies to serious fraud—not automated instant bans without process.

---

## B&C Lane Sellers

Reduced fees do **not** relax rules. Competition audio categories attract mislabeled power handling and used-as-new claims—list accurately.

---

## If You Made a Mistake

1. Fix the listing immediately if pre-sale
2. Message the buyer honestly if post-sale
3. Contact support with order ID if you need guidance
4. Do not ask the buyer to cancel off-platform to "work it out"
