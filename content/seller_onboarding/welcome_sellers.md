# Welcome, Sellers

Welcome to **The Franks Standard**—an owner-operated multi-vendor marketplace built for **authentic gear**. You're joining sellers who list collectibles, audio equipment, specialty items, and—through the B&C Performance Audio lane—competition sound gear with the same trust rails.

---

## What You Get

- **Buyers who checkout on-platform** — Stripe-powered payments, not forum DMs
- **COA chain-of-custody** for eligible categories — hashed uploads linked to listings and orders
- **Fraud monitoring** — off-platform payment requests and counterfeit patterns are flagged
- **Structured disputes** — defined response windows and escalation
- **Louisiana-compliant tax** — calculated from the buyer's shipping ZIP at checkout
- **Clear fees** — default platform fee is 10%; featured, starter, and B&C qualified plans may differ

---

## Before You List

1. **Create your account** and complete seller verification steps.
2. **Connect Stripe** in Seller Settings — payouts go to your connected account per schedule.
3. **Read seller policies** — authenticity, messaging, shipping, and prohibited items.
4. **Choose the right category** — COA and photo rules vary.

---

## Your Responsibilities (Short Version)

| Area | Expectation |
|------|-------------|
| Listings | Accurate title, condition, photos, serials when applicable |
| COA | Upload when category requires it |
| Shipping | Trackable methods where policy requires; pack securely |
| Messages | Professional; no off-platform payment requests |
| Disputes | Respond within your window with evidence |

---

## B&C Performance Audio Sellers

If you sell competition audio, you may qualify for **reduced platform fees** in the B&C lane. You still use the same checkout, tax engine, and dispute system as every TFS seller—only marketing presentation and fee qualification differ.
