# The Franks Standard — Founder Story

## Where we came from

The Franks Standard was built in Louisiana by collectors who were tired of buying blind. Too many marketplaces treat authenticity as an afterthought — a disclaimer in fine print after the money is already gone. We started TFS because the hobby deserves better than “buyer beware.”

## Our mission: eliminate fake products

Every listing on The Franks Standard is expected to carry proof — a Certificate of Authenticity (COA), a signed seller guarantee, or documented grading — before it reaches buyers. We did not build another auction site. We built a **proof-first marketplace** where counterfeit culture is not welcome.

## Protecting buyers and sellers

Buyers get escrow-backed checkout, dispute tools, and a platform that scans messages and reports for fraud patterns. Sellers get a fair fee structure, a **Starter Free 90** store plan for new shops, and protection from bad-faith buyers through verified orders and structured dispute resolution.

We protect both sides because a healthy marketplace cannot survive when only one party is safe.

## Why authenticity matters

Collectibles, coins, cards, and gear often carry emotional and financial weight. A fake item is not a “good deal gone wrong” — it is theft with extra steps. Authenticity is how trust scales online. Without it, every transaction is a gamble.

## Why TFS exists

TFS exists to be the place where:

- Proof is required before publish, not argued after delivery.
- Platform fees fund real enforcement, not just hosting.
- Louisiana marketplace facilitator rules are followed correctly — including tax on **shipping destination**, not billing address.

## Why TFS is safer than other marketplaces

| Typical marketplace | The Franks Standard |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| List first, prove later | Proof gate before publish |
| Message scams tolerated | Violation scanner + messaging freeze |
| Counterfeits handled case-by-case | Zero-tolerance policy + COA chain-of-custody |
| Opaque bans | Documented freeze, fraud case, and ban pipeline |
| No seller onboarding support | 90-day free store plan + daily spotlight for active shops |

We are not perfect — no platform is. But we built enforcement into the product, not the FAQ.

*— Charles Franks, Founder*
