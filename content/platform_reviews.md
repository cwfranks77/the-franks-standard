# Platform Reviews — How TFS Ratings Work

## How users rate TFS

Registered users may submit **platform reviews** after accepting marketplace terms and monitoring consent. Reviews include:

- Star rating (1–5)
- Written feedback (optional but encouraged)
- Optional evidence attachments (screenshots, order IDs) stored securely

Platform reviews are separate from seller/buyer transaction reviews.

## How ratings improve service

Aggregate ratings and comments feed owner dashboards and support quality metrics. Follow-up emails after closed tickets and disputes include their own 1–5 scores — together they show where the platform succeeds or fails.

We use this signal to prioritize:

- Checkout friction fixes
- Dispute response times
- Policy clarity updates
- Scanner rule tuning (without exposing private user data)

## How feedback is used

- **Public aggregates** may be displayed as summary stats (average rating, review count).
- **Individual reviews** may be quoted in marketing only with permission.
- **Abusive or false reviews** are not used for public promotion.

Internal ops can flag reviews linked to fraud cases.

## How abusive reviews are filtered

Every platform review passes the **AI violation scanner** before publication. Content that includes threats, hate speech, harassment, illegal instructions, or off-platform contact is blocked or triggers enforcement.

Repeat abuse can result in messaging freezes, account freezes, or bans — the same pipeline used for messages and disputes.

## Transaction reviews (sellers & buyers)

| Type | Who can review | Requirement |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| Seller review | Buyer | Verified purchase on that order |
| Buyer review | Seller | Verified purchase on that order |
| Platform review | Registered user | Terms + monitoring consent |

Your voice matters — keep it factual, and we keep the marketplace clean.
